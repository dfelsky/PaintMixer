Nearest mix
Tube colours approximate Golden Heavy Body masstones. Mixing is estimated with a two-constant Kubelka–Munk model that accounts for each pigment's opacity: transparent phthalos and quinacridones punch far above their volume, which is why pale tints can call for 20+ parts of white. Always test a small swatch before committing. A “part” is any consistent measure: a pea-sized blob, a knife-tip, a drop.
